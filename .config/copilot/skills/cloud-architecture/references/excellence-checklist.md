# Excellence Checklist

## Architecture Excellence Checklist

- Meet availability and resilience targets (e.g., 99.99%).
- Validate multi-region or multi-zone resilience and failover.
- Achieve cost optimization targets and enforce budget controls.
- Enforce security by design and verify compliance requirements.
- Adopt Infrastructure as Code and versioned configuration.
- Document architectural decisions and operational runbooks.
- Test disaster recovery plans and recovery objectives.
- Train teams and enable continuous improvement.
